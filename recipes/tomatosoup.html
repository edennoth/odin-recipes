Tomato Soup and Grilled Cheese

Description

One of our household favorites is this Grilled Cheese and Tomato Soup recipe!

You can thank Chef Billy for this recipe!

Home

Ingredients

  • 2-28 oz cans of San Marzano Tomatoes
  • Kosher Salt
  • Pepper
  • One medium-diced Yellow onion
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons Balsamic Vinegar
  • 1.5 tablespoons of Sugar
  • 1 cup of Chicken Broth
  • 3 cloves of minced garlic
  • 1 cup Heavy Cream
  • Brioche bread
  • Sliced Gouda, Provolone, and Cheddar

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a large Dutch Oven over mediumish heat
  2. Once butter has fully melted, add in your diced onions and cook until soft/tranlucent; about 5-6 minutes
  3. Once onions are soft, add in your sugar and cook until browned, stirring occassionally; about 7-10 minutes
  4. Add in garlic and cook until fragrant; approximately 1-2 minutes
  5. Pour in tomatoes and stew for about 6 minutes, then add in the chicken stock and then the balsamic vinegar
  6. For the next step, you can either blend in a separate container or use an immulsion blender in the dutch oven
  7. Once the mixture is smooth, transfer back to the dutch oven if you used a blender, turn the heat low and add in the cream, salt and sugar
  8. In a separate pan, heat butter over medium heat until melted and then add in your slices of bread
  9. Once the first sides are toasted to your liking, flip and add cheese and stack the toasted sides against the cheese
  10. Flip every 1-2 minutes until cooked; cover if needed between flips
  11. Repeat steps 9-11 for each grilled cheese, usig the same pan
